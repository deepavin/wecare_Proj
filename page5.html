Our Vision and Mission 

We Care is a platform that tracks waste created by Residents, Commercials,
Communities across the world with a goal to help our communities to track
& reduce the waste generated by them for a sustainable future. Our
Intuitive real-time dashboard reports provide trash-generating location
information using GeoMaps with Heat Maps highlighting Trash locations. Our
goal is to reduce the landfills by following 3 steps: RTC Recycle as much
as possible, lessen your Trash, and Compost organic waste. Use separate
Trash Cans for recycling, Compost, Trash & Hazardous wastes.

Conduct various events with the communities, Connect Communities with local Government programs to help them achieve greener environments. Create awareness among today's young generation on the amount of waste generated by them over a period of time. Educate people, Communities with more information helping them implement segregation of waste. We Care focuses on educating people about the consumption of reusable products and educating the young generation about the responsibilities for a sustainable future by conducting awareness programs in schools and communities.
